## Summary

Review and align the visible copy, framing, and layout of the three demo apps so the shared baseline story is consistent while still allowing framework-specific emphasis where it clarifies the point of each app.

## Prompt

Review the content and copy of the apps, align them where it makes sense, keep intentional framework-specific differences where they help, and align stylesheet/layout differences as necessary.

## Requirements

- Review the visible copy and framing in `boxes-web`, `boxes-angular`, and `boxes-react`.
- Align shared control descriptions where the behavior and purpose are the same.
- Keep framework-specific wording only where it clarifies the Angular or React angle.
- Align stylesheet/layout differences where they are accidental rather than intentional.
- Verify the affected apps still build after the copy/layout updates.
